Practice Questions

Math Practice Set 1

Try these questions after studying the notes.

  • 1. Solve: 3x + 5 = 14
  • 2. Find the perimeter of a rectangle with length 8 cm and width 5 cm.
  • 3. Simplify: 2(3x - 4) + 5

Science Practice Set 1

Check your understanding of basic science topics.

  • 1. Define speed and write its formula.
  • 2. Name any three renewable sources of energy.
  • 3. What is the function of the cell membrane?

English Practice Set 1

Improve your grammar and writing skills.

  • 1. Identify the verb in this sentence: "The dog ran quickly."
  • 2. Rewrite this sentence in the past tense: "She writes a letter."
  • 3. Write a short paragraph about your favorite hobby.
