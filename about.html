About This Website

A simple and friendly digital learning space for school students.

The Student E-Learning Hub is designed to make learning easier and more enjoyable for school students. Here you can quickly access:

  • Clear and printable notes for your main subjects.
  • Helpful video lessons that explain important topics.
  • Practice questions and quizzes to check your understanding.

This website uses only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which makes it very easy to update. Teachers or students can add new notes, videos, or questions by simply copying and editing the existing cards in the HTML files.

The goal is to support self-study and revision, whether you are at school, at home, or on the go using your phone or tablet.
